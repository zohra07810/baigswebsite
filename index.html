What is AR?

Augmented Reality (AR) is a technology that overlays digital 3D elements onto the real world through your device’s camera. It blends virtual and real environments, allowing you to interact with digital content in a more immersive way.

WebAR takes this a step further by bringing AR experiences directly through a web browser, no app download needed.

Bringing AR into dining introduces a whole new kind of experience in the food industry. It lets diners see their dishes come to life in 3D before ordering. This is especially helpful for foods that are uncommon or unfamiliar, giving customers a clearer idea of what to expect.
